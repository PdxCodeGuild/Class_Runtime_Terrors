Josip Grbavac (born in November 1967), better known as Braco (Croatian: bratso),
sometimes called "the Gazer", is a Croatian self-styled "healer". He does not touch,
speak to, diagnose, or treat the people who come to see him, but rather, he stands on a
platform and gazes.
