Hawaii

Kaneohe Marine Corps Base

Kaneohe Marine Corps Base Hawaii is home to a handful of great surfing beaches. Pyramid Rock and North Beach span over a mile beach with around a dozen different waves. During the winter time, large north swells will consistently wrap into the beaches. Throughout the entire year east winds drive tradewind swells as well, making the beaches very consistent for surfing activities. However, the best wind conditions occur in the winter, when occassional "Kona winds", or winds from the south, blow over the island.

Hickam Air Force Base

Hickam AFB has a wave called "Runways" located off the airport runway. The wave only breaks on large summer South and Southwest swells. Conditions are usually choppy and rough as the wave is pretty exposed to sideshore winds. The crowd is often minimal as it can be a very long paddle out to the break, but if you are willing to wait for the ferry you can pay a few dollars to be shuttled out to the surf zone
