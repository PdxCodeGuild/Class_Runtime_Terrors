Oscar, the Rose of Versaille

A Lady-Fencer in the Court of Marie-Antoinette

Oscar is the fifth daughter of a Count who is determined to have at least one son. On hearing that his child will be a girl, he goes a little crazy and declares that she is a boy. He names her Oscar and raises her as a son, keeping her true gender a secret. At the age of 14, Oscar wins her first duel to become a junior Commander of the Royal Guards... but even though she has made her fatherproud, she begins to feel the pain of having to deny her own femininity. Her first military assignment is to escort Marie-Antoinette to the French Court. Marie is about her own age. They soon become friends, each envying the other.
